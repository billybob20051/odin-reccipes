Its cheese, sauce, and bread put together.

You throw the dough up into the air and spin it so its nice and floofy.

Then marinate the dough with sauce being careful to evenly spread it.

Finally, sprinkles the cheese on top and cook it. its good

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • sauce
  • dough

Steps

  1. Roll dough
  2. spread sauce
  3. sprinkle cheese
  4. cook and serve
